Description

Breast Cancer Diagnosis and Prognosis is a project aimed at improving early detection and outcome prediction of breast cancer using data-driven techniques. The project addresses the challenge of delayed or inaccurate diagnosis by applying computational analysis to patient clinical data to classify tumors as benign or malignant and to estimate disease progression.
By leveraging machine learning models, the system enhances diagnostic accuracy, supports clinical decision-making, and helps healthcare professionals identify high-risk cases early. This approach promotes timely treatment, improves patient prognosis, and contributes to more efficient and reliable breast cancer management.
